  • Arakura Fuji Sengen Shrine #attraction 💖
    • famous spot to view Fujisan, Shinto shrine with a 5-tiered pagoda & an observation deck offering panoramic vistas of Mount Fuji.
    • 📍2-chome-4-1 Asama, Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi 403-0031, Japan

  • Chureito Pagoda #attraction 💖
    • Iconic pagoda on a hilltop facing Mt. Fuji in Arakurayama Sengen Park, reached via 398 steps.
    • 2-chome-4-1 Asama, Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi 403-0011, Japan

  • Lake kawaguchiko #attraction 💖
    • Second-largest of the Fuji Five Lakes, this scenic getaway is known for fishing, camping & hiking.
    • 📍Fujikawaguchiko, Minamitsuru District, Yamanashi, Japan
